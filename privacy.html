Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At Metdesigners, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our services and content automation tools.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our services, including account credentials for connected social media platforms (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, X, Pinterest), video content and metadata submitted for publishing, usage data to improve our platform, and contact information such as email address provided voluntarily.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to provide and improve our services — to authenticate and connect to your social media accounts, process and distribute your content to connected platforms, and communicate with you regarding your account or service updates.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Content and metadata are processed temporarily during distribution. We do not permanently store your video content after successful distribution. Account credentials and tokens are stored securely and used only for service delivery. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Our platform integrates with TikTok, Instagram (Meta), YouTube (Google), X (Twitter), and Pinterest. When you connect these accounts, you are also subject to their respective privacy policies. We only share data with these platforms to the extent necessary to deliver content on your behalf. We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction, including encrypted storage of API credentials and restricted access to user data.

7. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze site traffic. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect certain features.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time, and to withdraw consent for data processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at metdesignersclub@gmail.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.
